University of Auckland Fighting Game Club

About Us

The University of Auckland Fighting Game Club is a platform for newcomers and veterans to the fighting game community to enjoy fighting games in a casual and competitive environment!

Events

We host casual and competitive events for players of all skill levels. Our main series is the Kumite line, which is a social night featuring fighting games of all sorts!

In addition, the UoAFGC hosts the premier Smash 4 monthly - Limit To Win It, and we are open to more ideas for competitive ranking events!
